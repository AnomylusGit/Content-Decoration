Cricket

is a bat and ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a oval-shaped field at the centre of which is a 
22-yard pitch
with a wicket at each end, consisting of two bails balanced on three stumps. The bating side scores runs by striking the ball bowled at the wicket with the bat while the bowling and fielding side tries to prevent this and
dismiss or get batsmen out by catching balls in the air, by runouts and bowling the stumps leading to the batter being out!
